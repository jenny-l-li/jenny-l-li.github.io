VOLLEYBALLSERVES.

MATHEMATICA DEMONSTRATION

VIEW ON WOLFRAM DEMONSTRATIONS

Created a simulation of a volleyball serve using Mathematica.

DESCRIPTION

This Demonstration shows the projectile motion behind a volleyball serve. You can vary the height and angle of the serve. Press the play button to watch the ball move and try to make the ball go across the net. Press "reset ball" to reset the ball and player's initial position.
