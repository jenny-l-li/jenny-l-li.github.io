PHONE APP PROTOYPE.

UX DESIGN

Designed a prototype using Adobe Xd for a music and artist-sharing app. This is what I did with the project.

DESCRIPTION

This app primarily functions as a music-sharing app that puts more focus on facilitating users' exploration of new artists and keep up with news and recent releases of that artist. In addition, users can read comments or others or see friends' activity and recently played songs to encourages them to discover more artists.

FEATURES

  • Unique artist pages featuring their most popular songs and recent albums
  • Search feature for artist or song
  • Calendar of your artists' releases, news, and events
  • Store for merchandise
